Ice Age Card List
Wizards of the Coast, Inc.
(Released June 1995)

383 Cards Total (56 Black, 56 Blue, 56 Green, 56 Red, 56 White, 
25 Multicolor, 45 Artifacts, 33 Land; 20 Land, 121 Commons, 121 
Uncommons, 121 Rare)

Explanation of Rarity: the number after the rarity (e.g.: Common 1), 
means that that card appears that many times on the print sheet. 

The following Card Lists are also available for Magic: The Gathering: 
Alpha/Beta/Unlimited, Arabian Nights, Antiquities, Legends, 
The Dark, Fallen Empires, Revised, 4th Edition, Chronicles, 
Homelands, Alliances, Mirage, Visions, and 5th Edition..

Mana Key: 
U - Blue, W - White, G - Green, B - Black, R - Red; 
Numbers - Colorless (Generic) Mana

Note: n/a means "not applicable"
