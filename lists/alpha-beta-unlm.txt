Alpha/Beta/Unlimited Card List
Wizards of the Coast, Inc.
(Alpha released August 1993, Beta released October 1993, Unlimited
Released December 1993)

Alpha (295 cards total), Beta/Unlimited (302 cards total)

The following cards are not in the Alpha Series: 
Circle of Protection: Black, Forest (ver. 3), Island (ver. 3), 
Mountain (ver. 3), Plains (ver. 3), Swamp (ver. 3), Volcanic Island

Explanation of Rarity: the number after the rarity (e.g.: Common 1),
means that that card appears that many times on the print sheet. 

The following Card Lists are also available for Magic: The Gathering: 
Promo Cards, Arabian Nights, Antiquities, Legends, The Dark, 
Fallen Empires, Revised, 4th Edition, Ice Age, Chronicles, Homelands, 
Alliances, Mirage, Visions, and 5th Edition.

Mana Key: 
U - Blue, W - White, G - Green, B - Black, R - Red; 
Numbers - Colorless (Generic) Mana

Note: n/a means "not applicable"
