Legends Card List
Wizards of the Coast, Inc.
(Released June 1994)

310 Cards Total (43 Black, 43 Blue, 43 Green, 43 Red, 43 White, 55 
Multicolor, 29 Artifacts, 11 Lands; 121 Rare, 114 Uncommon, 75 Common)

Explanation of Rarity: the number after the rarity (e.g.: Common 1), 
means that that card appears that many times on the print sheet. 

The following Card Lists are also available for Magic: The Gathering: 
Alpha/Beta/Unlimited, Promo Cards, Arabian Nights, Antiquities,
The Dark, Fallen Empires, Revised, 4th Edition, Ice Age, Chronicles,
Homelands, Alliances, Mirage,and 5th Edition.

Mana Key: 
U - Blue, W - White, G - Green, B - Black, R - Red; Numbers - Colorless 
(Generic) Mana

Note: n/a means "not applicable"
