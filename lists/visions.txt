Visions Card List
Wizards of the Coast, Inc.
(Released Febuary 3rd)

167 Cards Total (25 Black, 25 Blue, 25 Green, 25 Red, 25 White, 
15 Multicolor, 19 Artifacts, 8 Land; 62 Commons, 55 Uncommons, 
50 Rare)

Explanation of Rarity: the number after the rarity (e.g.: Common 1), 
means that that card appears that many times on the print sheet. 

The following Card Lists are also available for Magic: The Gathering: 
Alpha/Beta/Unlimited, Promo Cards, Arabian Nights, Antiquities, 
Legends, The Dark, Fallen Empires, Revised, 4th Edition, Ice Age, 
Chronicles, Homelands, Alliances, Mirage,and 5th Edition.

Mana Key: 
U - Blue, W - White, G - Green, B - Black, R - Red; 
Numbers - Colorless (Generic) Mana

Note: n/a means "not applicable"
