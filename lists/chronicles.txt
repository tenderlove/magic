Chronicles Card List
Wizards of the Coast, Inc.
(Released September 1995)

125 Cards Total (14 Black, 14 Blue, 14 Green, 14 Red, 14 White, 20
Multicolor, 21 Artifacts, 14 Land; 54 Commons, 71 Uncommons, 50 Rare;
14 from Arabian Nights, 21 from Antiquities, 71 from Legends, 19 from
The Dark)

Explanation of Rarity: the number after the rarity (e.g.: Common 1), means 
that that card appears that many times on the print sheet. 

The following Card Lists are also available for Magic: The Gathering: 
Alpha/Beta/Unlimited, Promo Cards, Arabian Nights, Antiquities, Legends,
The Dark, Fallen Empires, Revised, 4th Edition, Ice Age,
Homelands, Alliances, Mirage, Visions, and 5th Edition.

Mana Key: 
U - Blue, W - White, G - Green, B - Black, R - Red; Numbers - Colorless 
(Generic) Mana

Note: n/a means "not applicable"
