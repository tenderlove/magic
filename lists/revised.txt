Revised (3rd Edition) Card List
Wizards of the Coast, Inc.
(Released April 1994)

306 Cards Total (46 White, 45 Green, 46 Black, 46 Blue, 47 Red, 
25 Land, 51 Artifacts; 121 Rare, 95 Uncommon, 75 Commons, 
15 Lands)

Explanation of Rarity: the number after the rarity (e.g.: common 1), 
means that that card appears that many times on the print sheet. 

The following Card Lists are also available for Magic: The Gathering: 
Alpha/Beta/Unlimited, Promo Cards, Arabian Nights, Antiquities, 
Legends, The Dark, Fallen Empires, 4th Edition, Ice Age, 
Chronicles, Homelands, Alliances, Mirage,Visions, and 5th Edition.

Mana Key: 
U - Blue, W - White, G - Green, B - Black, R - Red; Numbers - 
Colorless (Generic) Mana

Note: n/a means "not applicable"
